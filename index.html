Description

Introducing "EcoAlertGostivar: Your Environmental Companion" your all-in-one solution for environmental awareness and action. With EcoAlertGostivar, you can stay informed about air quality, temperature, and report environmental issues like wildfires, full containers, and illegal dumpings with ease. Key Features:

  1. Real-Time AQI and Weather Updates: Stay informed about the air quality index (AQI) and temperature in Gostivar at your fingertips. Receive up-to-date information to plan your outdoor activities and protect your health.
  2. Report Environmental Issues: Be a part of the solution! With EcoAlertGostivar, you can report wildfires, full containers, and illegal dumpings directly from your smartphone. Help keep your community clean and safe.
  3. EcoAlertGostivar Awareness: EcoAlertGostivar is your companion in environmental stewardship. Raise ecological awareness and take meaningful action to preserve the natural beauty of your surroundings.
  4. Garbage Truck Service: Need to schedule a garbage pickup? EcoAlertGostivar offers a convenient service to request a garbage truck right from the app. Say goodbye to overflowing bins and hello to a cleaner neighborhood.
Join the EcoAlertGostivar community today and make a difference for a greener, healthier planet. Together, we can protect our environment for generations to come. Download EcoAlertGostivar now and take the first step towards a sustainable future.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information obtained from users of our app.

Information We Collect:

  • Photos: We may collect photos taken by users for the purpose of reporting wildfires, illegal dumpings, and full containers.
  • Location Data: We may collect the geographic location of the user at the time of reporting for the purpose of accurately identifying the incident location.

Use of Information:

The information collected by our app is used solely for the purpose of reporting wildfires, illegal dumpings, and full containers. We do not share this information with third parties.

Data Security:

We take appropriate measures to ensure the security of user information and prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

User Controls:

Users have the option to delete photos and location data from their device after reporting an incident. Users can also contact us at support@yourapp.com to request the deletion of their data from our records.

Legal Compliance:

We comply with all relevant privacy laws and regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
