Privacy Policy for EcoAlertGostivar

This Privacy Policy explains how EcoAlertGostivar collects, uses, and protects the information obtained from users of the app.

Information We Collect

  • Photos: EcoAlertGostivar may collect photos taken by users for the purpose of reporting wildfires, illegal dumpings, and full containers. This is not mandatory. The user can choose wether to upload a photo.
  • Location Data: EcoAlertGostivar may collect the geographic location of the user at the time of reporting for the purpose of accurately identifying the incident location. This is not mandatory. The user can choose wether to use current location or manually write an address.

Use of Information

The information collected by EcoAlertGostivar is used solely for the purpose of reporting wildfires, illegal dumpings, and full containers. We do not share this information with third parties.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure the security of user information and prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of data.

User Controls

Users have the option to delete photos and location data from their device after reporting an incident. Users can also contact us at [Your Contact Information] to request the deletion of their data from our records.

Legal Compliance

EcoAlertGostivar complies with all relevant privacy laws and regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
